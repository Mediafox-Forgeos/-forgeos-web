# Company Documentation

This directory is the institutional home of MediaFOX Forge. It contains founding documents, organizational structure, operating principles, and company-wide standards.

All documents here apply to every product, every agent, and every team member operating under the MediaFOX Forge name.

---

## Documents

| Document | Status | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Founder Team Constitution](./FOUNDER_TEAM_CONSTITUTION.md) | Active | Founding organizational structure, executive roles, engineering principles, product strategy, and AI collaboration principles |
| Company Overview | Planned | Public-facing description of MediaFOX Forge: what we build, who we serve, and why |
| Organization | Planned | Full organizational chart as the team grows beyond the founding stage |
| Employee Handbook | Planned | Operating standards for all team members: processes, communication, tooling, and expectations |

---

## Scope

Documents in this directory answer questions like:

- Who owns what?
- How do we make decisions?
- What principles guide our work?
- What is the company building and for whom?
- How do humans and AI agents collaborate?

Technical architecture decisions belong in [`docs/architecture/`](../architecture/) or [`docs/adr/`](../adr/).  
Engineering standards belong in [`docs/engineering/`](../engineering/).  
Product specifications belong in [`docs/product/`](../product/).  
Agent protocols belong in [`docs/agents/`](../agents/).
